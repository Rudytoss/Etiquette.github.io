Meet the Founder

Ms. Jasmine Guy founded Royalty School of Etiquette in the Spring of 2017. Royalty School of Etiquette is a program created to empower young ladies and gentlemen. Ms. Jasmine Guy is a certified etiquette professional, life coach, mentor, motivational speaker, minister and author. In 2014, Ms. Guy created the Princess Outreach for young ladies, and in 2016 she recognized the need for young men to possess good etiquette and social skills as well. Today, Royalty School of Etiquette offers a variety of classes geared toward the betterment of young men and women.
