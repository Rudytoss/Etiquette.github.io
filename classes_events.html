Bud

Royalty School of Etiquette offers several classes to help with the progression of your precious prince or princess. We like to start our students off with beginner level courses such as Lanugage and Self-Esteem.


Flower

As the prince and princess progress within the program, they will be able to obtain the following skills: Commitment, Behavioral, and Walking/Speech.


Rose

As the program approaches the end, the prince and princess are going to continue to grow and attain the following skills: Attire,Posture, Basic Dancing, and Self Discipline.


Bouquet

A collaboration of the fundamentals in all of my offered classes in which the prince and princess are going to graduate with a sense of brotherhood and sisterhood for their fellow classmates. They will have also, collectively, learned dining, self-esteem, attire, posture, language, self-discipline, commitment, walking/speech, behavioral, and basic dancing skills. These skills will help to create greater character into our growing princes and princesses.
